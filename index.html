About Forge

Coaching first.
Everything else follows.

Forge started in 2016 as a single studio with one barbell rack and a simple idea: people get stronger faster when someone qualified is watching their bar path, not their step count. A decade later we run three studios, but the ratio never changed — small groups, real coaching, programs built around your actual numbers.

  • ✓ Every member starts with a strength assessment and a 12-week program, not a generic template.
  • ✓ Coach-to-member ratio capped at 1:8 in every session, every time.
  • ✓ Progress logged after every session so you can see the trend, not just the day.

Why Forge

What actually makes a program work.

It's not the equipment. It's the structure around it.

Certified coaches, on the floor

Every session is led by a nationally certified strength coach — never an app on a screen.

Capped class sizes

No more than eight members per coach, so your form gets checked every set, not once a class.

Progress you can see

Every lift, set, and rep is logged in your member app, charted against your 12-week plan.

Dedicated recovery zone

Mobility tools, compression, and stretch stations included with every membership tier.

Community events

Monthly total nights, mobility workshops, and team-based conditioning challenges.

Flexible scheduling

Sessions run six days a week from 5:30am to 8pm — book, reschedule, or cancel from the app.
